This new battle royale from the South Korean company KRAFTON, Inc. gives you the chance to experience exhilarating battles
where your only chance of winning is to be the last survivor. Countless rivals will try to catch you by surprise
as you progress on an island that's full of dangerous elements.





However, you'll find plenty of vehicles and other elements to equip your character well-enough to be able to stand a chance against
any opponent. You'll gradually get to explore every corner of a vast map that holds more dangers than it
may seem at first. And who knows? You just might manage to sneak into the top positions of the game's global leaderboards.
